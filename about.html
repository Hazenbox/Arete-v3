Chaos Fades… Calm Unfolds….

Dr. Ramya Seeni, M.D, Psychiatry

Pet mom, mother of 2, a trauma survivor. That’s my story. We all have a story. Your story is important; YOU are important. 

Whether you are a career driven individual with ADHD, a professional with job related anxiety, an entrepreneur with bipolar disorder, a new mother with postpartum depression, a homemaker silently suffering, in a relationship with issues- we can work together. I am only a resource that can help you be a better version of yourself; so, there is no disruption in your relationships- be it friend, family or significant other. As a licensed Adult Psychiatrist, I have experience treating patients from 13-103 during my residency training as well as working in a county hospital. While working at skilled nursing facilities and senior living homes, intrigued me on the complex circle of life, working at the Federal Bureau of Prisons helped me hone my skills in Forensic Psychiatry. If you are looking for either medications or therapy, we are not a good fit. If you are looking for both, you are in the right place.
