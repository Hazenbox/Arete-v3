Autism spectrum disorder
Autism Spectrum disorder

It's crucial to recognize the diversity of experiences within the autism spectrum. Some individuals may experience greater challenges, while others may excel in certain areas. I embrace the concept of neurodiversity and emphasize on the importance of recognizing and embracing the differences in cognitive functioning and sensory processing.

I use the RAADS- R scale to evaluate your experiences during social interactions.

This approach allows me to tailor interventions based on the patient's developmental age rather than chronological age, contributing to a smoother and more tailored treatment process.

PTSD & EMDR- Trauma Recovery
PTSD & EMDR- Trauma Recovery

Emotions pushed to the back seat, now overpowering you by taking the steering wheel?

Medications have proven efficacy for acute PTSD; Chronic PTSD requires a targeted approach to rewire the brain, and I am adeptly trained in evidence-based techniques such as Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). These methods have proven efficacy in promoting healing and reducing stress and stress related neuronal death.

Genetic Testing
Genetic Testing

Multiple medication failures, minimal to no improvement in mood, feeling weary of being treated as a guinea pig treated are some reasons to have genetic testing done. These tests need to be ordered via special labs and are covered by insurance for most part.

Genetic testing can help me provide you with personalized medication treatments, predict responses to specific medications, and optimize your medication regimen.

Balanced Nutrition & Supplements
Balanced Nutrition & Supplements

Supplements, plant-based medicine, and nutraceutical treatments are excellent complements or alternatives to medications. Oftentimes, these interventions allow us to use medication for a shorter time, or be able to use lower doses. We also provide and interpret functional lab testing to better investigate nutrient deficiencies and root causes of complicated or distressing symptoms
