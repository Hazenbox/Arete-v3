Exceptional psychiatry

Welcome! As licensed Psychiatrists, we are passionate about providing Arête Mental Health services to our patients. We mine the fuel from evidence-based medicine, refine it with good publications, and research and custom make the treatment plans for you based on your bio-psycho-socio-economic-nutritional needs, to get the human engine running. We believe, "There is hope, even when your brain tells you there isn't."

Chemical Dependency Services

"One Drink is too many, & a thousand is never enough..." - Gregory Forrest

Addiction has 4 components as described by SAMHSA: 2 genetic components which are unmodifiable, and 2 modifiable components - Environment and Experience.

At Arête Psychiatry, we work on these modifiable components to achieve abstinence and maintain it. Recovery is a path where relapse is a rule.

Arête services offered include: Smoking Cessation, Alcohol use disorder, Opioid use disorder, and Cannabis use disorder.

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

TMS is a noninvasive treatment that allows us to stimulate structures located deep within the mood and craving centers of the brain that have been linked to depression, OCD, and smoking cessation. Click here to find out if you are a candidate for this. Insurance from the following providers accepted for the TMS through Headway only: Cigna, Oxford, United Healthcare, Oscar Health, Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Texas, Aetna.

Esketamine

While ketamine is a commonly abused drug, and not approved by FDA, Esketamine, a nasal spray, is used in treatment-resistant depression, people with acute suicidal thoughts and is FDA approved. The risks and benefits of this office administered medication are highly subjective and will need to be supervised closely. At Arête Psychiatry, we will try to work with you to make these secular trends into conventional pathways.
